Welcome to Gainz App

Created for hodler's who need some clarity

Gainz Mission Statement: Help people solve the "I don't know what I've done with my crypto" problem :-)


After a unsuccessful attempt in excel, and finding nothing that felt quite right I started working on Gainz.
Maybe you're in a similar situation trying remember what you did to make sense of it all. In anycase I want to say thank you for your interest and support.

Gainz is made to be used fully offline and disconnected from the internet to maintain privacy.

Gainz will import exchange transactions CSV(s) then guide you though some common scenarios to provide clarity and understanding of your crypto holdings and associated Gains or losses.

Get started by importing transactions then work your way down to export.

Reports will be exported in excel format for your reference and shareability.

Gainz, Does not provide Legal, Financial, or Tax Advice. Please consult with your tax advisor to validate results.
