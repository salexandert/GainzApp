Welcome to Gainz App

Created for hodler's who need some clarity

Gainz Mission Statement: Help people solve the "IDKWTF I've done with my crypto" problem :-)


After an nightmare attempt in excel, and finding nothing readily available I started working on Gainz.
Maybe this is you too, in anycase I want to say thank you for your interest and support.
Gainz is made to be used fully offline and disconnected from the internet to maintain privacy.

Gainz will import exchange transactions CSV(s) then guide you though some common scenarios to provide a clearer
understanding of your crypto holdings and associated gains or losses.

Reports can be exported in excel format for your reference and shareability.
